Packed full of features you'll love

Free Load Balancers

Need to load balance traffic across multiple cloud servers? Now you can do it with a click of a button, directly from our control panel. Billed at 2c per GB of network transfer (the first 1 TB is free).

Block Storage Volumes

Create an SSD block storage volume with a single click, and then attach it to any one of your cloud servers. Scale it up at any time. Great for databases and other high IO applications! Billed at $0.0001488 / GB / hour ($0.10 / GB / month).

Built-in Advanced Firewalls

Create security profiles with advanced firewall rules that can then be applied to any one of your cloud servers, in real-time, all manageable through our easy-to-use Motion control panel. Free of charge.

Auto Scaling

Create auto scaling profiles that create and destroy cloud servers based on either a time schedule, or resource alarms. In combination with load balancing, you can quickly scale up to meet demand, and scale down to reduce expenses.

Scheduling cloud backups

Keep your data safe. Create a one-time backup or schedule daily, weekly and monthly backups. Motion will automatically back up your cloud, compress it, and save it. You can restore it with just a single click. (10¢ per GB per month, billed hourly.)

Converting backups to cloud templates

Simplify your deployment process by converting a backup to a template that's deployable in any of our availability zones. User-created templates are accessible under "User Templates" in Motion. Upon cloud creation, just select your custom template. (10¢ per GB per month, billed hourly)

Rescuing your cloud

Motion's web console and rescue kernel tools may be useful if your operating system becomes inaccessible, if files are needing to be repaired or restored, or if your file system becomes damaged.

Build upon the Motion API

Utilize the Motion API to take complete control over your cloud environment and automate your infrastructure. Our API comes with easy-to-understand documentation and examples to get you started. Learn more.

Dashboards

Compare your target performances

REST APIs, DNS and more and be the first to know when your service is down.
